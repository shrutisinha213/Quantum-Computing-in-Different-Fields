# Research Paper on Quantum Computing in Different Fields

## Overview
This research paper explores the applications of Quantum Computing across multiple domains and industries.

## Topics Covered
- Quantum Computing Fundamentals
- Healthcare Applications
- Cybersecurity
- Finance and Banking
- Artificial Intelligence
- Future Technological Advancements

## Objective
The objective of this research is to study how quantum technologies can solve complex computational and optimization problems in various sectors.

## Applications
- Drug Discovery
- Secure Communication
- Financial Modeling
- AI Optimization
- Advanced Simulations
